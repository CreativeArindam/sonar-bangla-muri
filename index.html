What is puffed rice?

Puffed rice (Muri) is widely used as a breakfast or snack mainly in Indian subcontinent. It is made by heated demoisturized rice grains.

Nutritional Value

It is oil free. It has no fat and fills your stomach up. It has high iron contents and it is good for a gluten free diet

Did you know?

The common street foods like 'Jhalmuri' in Kolkata, 'Bhelpuri' in Mumbai and 'Churmuri' in Bangalore is made by puffed rice.

Who we are?
